Welcome

Plantee is an easy to use water tracking tool that can help you consistently monitor the amount of water you are giving your plants.

Monitoring

To care for your plants, you can add as many plants as you would like to your collection. Specify the amount of water they need, and the frequency of watering. You will be reminded to check your plants everyday. Once you have watered one, press the water droplet button.

Happy Planting!
